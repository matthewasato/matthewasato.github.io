Our Story

Logos is a company inspired by literature. Our love for stories and the adventures they take us on was the heart behind the start of this organization. Here at Logos, we attempt to support aspiring authors through 3 simple steps:
  1. Inspire
  2. Educate
  3. Support
Through the simple steps of our action plan, our mission is to provide the opportunity for every author to have the chance for their projects to see the finish line.

You can find many of our published books at the West Lafayette Public Library

Brief History

Logos was founded in 2025. Though it has only been a few months since its opening, our company has quickly becaome a leader in the local book publishing industry. Located in the heart of West Lafayette, Indiana, Logos has supported over 100 local authors in getting their works to the public.
