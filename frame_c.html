Our Story

Logos is a company inspired by literature. Our love for stories and the adventures they take us on was the heart behind the start of this organization. Here at Logos, we attempt to support aspiring authors through 3 simple steps:
  1. Inspire
  2. Educate
  3. Support

You can find many of our published books at the West Lafayette Public Library

Brief History

Logos was founded in 2001. Since then it has been a leader in the local book publishing industry.
